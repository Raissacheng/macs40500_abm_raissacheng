# Friendship Formation ABM

An agent-based model of close relationship formation using Mesa and Solara.

This project investigates how both **passive exposure** and **active similarity evaluation** contribute to the emergence of friendships and social clustering.

---

> How do close relationships form based on both passive and active social factors?

This model combines two major mechanisms from social psychology:

1. **Familiarity (Passive Exposure)**  
   Individuals become more likely to like others through repeated encounters and spatial proximity.

2. **Similarity (Active Evaluation)**  
   Individuals actively evaluate how similar others are to themselves, preferring those with similar traits.

The simulation explores how these two processes jointly produce emergent friendship networks and spatial social clusters.
